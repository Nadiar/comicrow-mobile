ComicRow connects to your self-hosted OPDS comic server and lets you browse and read comics on your phone or tablet — no syncing or full downloads required. Pages stream on demand so you can start reading immediately.

Features:

• OPDS 1.2 and 2.0 catalog browsing with search
• Streaming-first reading — pages load on demand via DiViNa manifests (OPDS 2.0) and PSE page streams (OPDS 1.2), with full archive download as a fallback
• Three reading modes — single page, double-page spread, and vertical scroll
• Per-server presets — set default reading mode and auto double-page in landscape per server
• Offline support — download comics for reading without a connection
• Read progress tracking — picks up where you left off
• RTL and LTR reading direction support
• Light, dark, and system theme options

ComicRow is designed for self-hosted OPDS servers like ComicOPDS, Komga, Kavita, and others that expose comics via the OPDS standard.
