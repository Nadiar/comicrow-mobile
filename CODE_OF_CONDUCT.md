# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Be respectful. Provide constructive feedback. Assume good intent.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, and trolling.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior can be reported to the project maintainer. Reports will be reviewed and may result in a temporary or permanent ban from the project.
